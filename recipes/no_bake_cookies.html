No Bake Cookies

Description

This is a quick and easy recipe that delivers in the peanut butter taste department. If you don't have a lot of time on your hands, but develop a sweet tooth, you've come to the right place.

Ingredients

  • 1 stick butter
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 tablespoons cocoa powder (optional)
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup peanut butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 2 1/2 cups quick oats
  • nuts and/or raisins (optional)

Steps

  1. Mix butter, sugar, cocoa, and milk
  2. Cook over low-medium heat
  3. Bring to a boil and boil for one minute
  4. Remove from heat and add peanut butter and vanilla
  5. Stir
  6. Add oats and continue to stir
  7. Let cool slightly to "dropping" stage
  8. Spoon out onto wax paper
