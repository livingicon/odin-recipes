Carmen's Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

The hardest thing to get right with a chocolate chip cookie is that they are soft and not flat. This recipe delivers every time.

Ingredients

  • 4 1/4 cups of flour minus 2 tablestoons
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 3 sticks softened butter
  • 1 1/4 cups sugar
  • 1 1/4 cups brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla
  • 12 ounces (package) of milk chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees fahrenheit
  2. Combine flour, baking soda, and baking powder and mix
  3. In a separate bowl, combine butter and sugar beat until creamed
  4. Stir eggs and vanilla into the creamed sugar
  5. Gradually stir the dry ingredients into the wet ingredients
  6. Add chocolate chips
  7. Drop dough on an ungreased cookie sheet--1/4 cup drops at least 2 inches apart
  8. Cook 10-14 minutes
